Summary:
This simulation compares three global-history branch predictors:
gpredict, gselect, and gshare. Each predictor uses a 4-bit Global
History Register, a 16-entry Branch History Table, and 2-bit saturating
counters.

The results show that all three predictors achieve approximately the
same accuracy on this nested-loop branch trace. The similar performance
is caused by aliasing from the limited 4-bit history length and the
specific PC bit patterns of the two branches in the benchmark.
